Common questions

I’ve never felt confident dancing. Is this really for me?

Yes. The course is specifically designed for people who feel stiff, awkward, or overthink every move. You start with very simple foundations and build up slowly.

What if I don’t have a lot of space?

Lessons are filmed with small living spaces in mind. You don’t need a studio—just a few feet of room to move.

How often is new content added?

New sessions are added roughly every 1–2 months, so there is always something fresh to explore and practice.

Can I go at my own pace?

Absolutely. You can pause, rewind, and repeat as many times as you want. Confidence comes from repetition and feeling your own body, not rushing through content.
